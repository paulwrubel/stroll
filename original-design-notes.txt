STROLL LANGUAGE
-----------

H, House, is your house. you start from there, and always start your stroll heading east
>>>>, walk forward, increment cell by distance
<<<<, walk backward, decrement cell by distance
0, no op, usually for turning
--- or |||, skip, no op
y, yell, print current cell as character
l/r, forks in the road. these are the only nodes that allow branching paths. they mean "go straight unless the current cell is zero, then go in that direction (left/right)
